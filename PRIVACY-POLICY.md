# Privacy Policy for Baby Screen-time

Effective date: 2025-11-01

We do not collect personal information.

- What we collect: Only app settings stored locally on your device that are necessary for the app’s functionality (for example: timer preferences, feature toggles, display and sound settings).
- What we do not collect: We do not collect, transmit, or store names, emails, contacts, location, analytics, advertising identifiers, device IDs, or any other personal information.
- Third parties / ads: The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or external services that collect user data.
- Data storage & retention: Any settings are stored locally on your device and are retained only as long as you keep the app or the device unless you delete them in the app or by uninstalling the app.
- Children’s privacy: The app is designed for parents to manage screen time. We do not collect information from children.
- Security: Because we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data stored on our servers.
- Contact: For questions about privacy, contact: lexsfolks (lexsfolks@gmail.com)

Thank you for using Baby Screen-time.
